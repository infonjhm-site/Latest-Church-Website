Print Ministry

Information about printed resources, bulletins, and design requests.

Purpose & Impact

Provides a tangible and lasting medium to share the gospel. Transcends geographical boundaries to reach a global audience and enables in-depth teaching, theological reflection, and spiritual guidance.

Benefits

Caters to readers of all spiritual levels and interests. Acts as a faith companion—offering encouragement and inspiration, and ensures continuous access to messages, unlike media with limited airtime.

‘SAJEEVA VAAKU’ – The Living Word

Meaning: ‘Sajeeva Vaaku’ = ‘Living Word’.

Launched: Initiated in 2010 as a print-based gospel outreach.

Vision: To widely distribute the gospel in printed form and serve as a regular source of faith-building messages. Founded from a vision of Bishop Dr. V. Rangaraju, it reflects his passion for making disciples of Christ.

Format & Frequency

Released once every two months, 'Sajeeva Vaaku' acts as the print counterpart to intermittent TV programs. The goal is to ensure easy and ongoing access to the Word of God for all readers.
