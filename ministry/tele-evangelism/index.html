Tele-Evangelism

Outreach via broadcast and televised ministry partners.

Biblical Command

Rooted in Mark 16:15 – “Go into all the world and preach the gospel...”, our tele-evangelism ministry follows the ancient mandate to carry the good news to every nation and household.

Historical Evolution of Gospel Preaching

From itinerant missionaries to the printing press, from radio to television, each era expanded the reach of the gospel. Television (since the 1930s) introduced visual storytelling to evangelism, and today the digital era uses the internet, social media and live streaming to connect instantly across the globe.

Rise of Televangelism

Televangelism gained traction because television reached into homes and carried emotional, visual messages directly to viewers. Pioneers such as Bishop Fulton Sheen used the medium to preach into living rooms worldwide, combining audio-visual engagement to inspire and connect.

Bishop Rangaraju’s Media Ministry

Motivated by a passion for deeper life experiences and biblical living, Bishop Rangaraju began broadcasting in 2004 on Citi Cable in Hindupur. Overcoming initial challenges, the ministry expanded to national platforms and used television as a tool for revival and church planting.

‘Sajeeva Swaram’ – The Living Word (TV Ministry)

‘Sajeeva Swaram’ aired on Subhavartha Channel during prime time and proved instrumental in revival, renewal and spiritual empowerment. It became a strategic tool for planting churches across Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka.

Church Planting Impact

New Jerusalem Harvest Ministries saw congregations formed from viewers. The Bangalore NJHM church was founded on November 22, 2009 by an initial congregation composed of 'Sajeeva Swaram' viewers. Today the Horamavu congregation focuses on worship, fellowship, discipleship and stewardship.

Digital Expansion: YouTube Ministry

Channel: New Jerusalem Harvest Ministries — featuring live services, programs and worship songs by Bishop Rangaraju, Sis. Glory Rani, and their children. The digital ministry relies on advanced technology, social media, and committed volunteers to extend reach.

Global Impact

NJHM's media efforts have reached audiences across the world, spreading messages of hope, healing and deliverance, and demonstrating the power of the Living God in the digital age.
