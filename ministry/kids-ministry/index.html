KINGDOM KIDS – NJHM Children’s Ministry

Biblical Foundation

Inspired by Matthew 19:13–15 – “Let the little children come to Me...” and reinforced by Mark 10:13–16 and Luke 18:15–17, KINGDOM KIDS reflects Jesus’ love and acceptance of children.

Purpose & Mission

Nurture spiritual growth in children. Align children with principles of the Kingdom of Heaven. Foster a safe, joyful, and faith-filled environment for kids.

Vacation Bible School (VBS)

Conducted annually in multiple languages (English, Telugu, and Kannada). Targets children aged 3 to 19 years and is organized in various villages with temporary camps and planned activities.

Innovative Teaching Approach

Uses rotational learning methods and incorporates:

  • 🎵 Action songs
  • 🎶 Vibrant music
  • 🏃 Outdoor activities
  • 📖 Inspiring Bible stories

Impact & Engagement

Results in powerful testimonies and spiritual transformation. Promotes questioning and learning through peer interaction. Supports children in understanding life’s big questions through faith.

Collaborative Spirit

NJHM welcomes partnerships with groups and churches and aims to expand outreach to minister to more children effectively.
