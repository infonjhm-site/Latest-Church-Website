About Our Church

A Spirit-filled community dedicated to authentic worship and meaningful service.

Founded with a vision to prepare the Bride for the New Jerusalem, our ministry has grown from a humble house fellowship into a vibrant spiritual home for families across the region.

We are committed to sharing the Gospel with compassion, excellence, and unwavering faith, ensuring that everyone who walks through our doors feels a sense of belonging and divine purpose.
