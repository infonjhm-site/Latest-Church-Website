New Jerusalem

About Church

Learn about New Jerusalem Church — our history, purpose, and the heartbeat behind our ministry.

Mission

Dedicated to the harvest, led by the Holy Spirit to prepare the Bride for the New Jerusalem.

Vision

Build a resilient local church rooted in God’s Word. Promote love, holiness, and true worship. See lives transformed through personal encounters with Jesus Christ.

Founding & Growth

Founded by Bishop Dr. V. Rangaraju with a vision for field missions. Began as a small Bible study group in 1999. The first official service was held on July 30, 2000 in Hindupur, Andhra Pradesh. It started with just one member — Sis. Glory Rani Rangaraju (his wife). The ministry grew from a house fellowship to a rented shed and eventually to a permanent, beautiful church. The ministry expanded to Hindupur, Bangalore, and nearby communities.

Spiritual Foundation

Every decision and direction is based on God’s will and Spirit-led guidance. The church's name and mission were inspired by Revelation 21 — "The New Jerusalem".

Church Identity

Initially named New Jerusalem Church with the tagline "Changing lives in the image of Christ". In 2015 the tagline was refined to: "A place to prepare like Christ".
