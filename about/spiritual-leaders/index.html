About the Founder

Bishop Dr. V. Rangaraju is the visionary leader of New Jerusalem Harvest Ministries (NJHM). He inspires hundreds of families in India and globally through faith and compassion. Born in Machilipatnam, Andhra Pradesh, his life began with a miraculous survival at birth and he is the eldest of three children to Vudumula Chandra Mohan Raju & Elizabeth Rani.

Call & Formation

His life has been marked by divine purpose and obedience to The Great Commission (John 15:8–14; Matthew 28:16–20). He graduated from Berean Baptist Bible College & Seminary, Bangalore and was deeply inspired by faith leaders such as Billy Graham, Bakht Singh, Joshua Daniel, and Paul Yungi Cho. In 1998 he was baptized by the Holy Spirit, a turning point that led to total dependence on God.

Family & Ordination

He is married to Sis. Glory Rani and is blessed with a daughter and a son. He was ordained as 'Reverend' by the Billy Graham Evangelical Association in 2003, received an Honorary Doctorate from A.P. Theological Seminary in 2008, and was awarded the 'Kristu Seva Ratna' by the Vizag City Pastor's Fellowship in 2010. In 2019 he was honored with the title 'Bishop' by the A.P. Pastors Fellowship & Theological College.

Ministry & Impact

Bishop Rangaraju leads NJHM with deep spiritual insight and practical guidance. His ministries include preaching, prayer ministry, power encounters, and pastoral care. He emphasizes holistic transformation—spiritual and social—and his teachings impact families across nations, promoting unity and discipleship.

Vision

He aims to build a New Jerusalem community of faith, hope, and love. His legacy is marked by faith, service, and community upliftment.
